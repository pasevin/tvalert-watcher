WHAT IT DOES

Everything you'd expect. Nothing you don't.

No dashboard, no inbox, no noise. It sits in your menu bar and taps you the moment something happens.

01
Real-time
Pushed over a live WebSocket connection the instant TradingView fires — not polled, not delayed.
02
Native to macOS
A real notification, a menu-bar badge, and an optional sound. Translucent, SF-typeset, feels like it ships with the OS.
03
One click → chart
Click any alert and land directly on that symbol's TradingView chart. The logo's already there waiting.
04
Never missed
Alerts that fire while you're offline are held by the relay and delivered the moment you reconnect.
05
Zero setup
Sign in, paste one URL into TradingView. No tunnel, no localhost, no port forwarding.
GETTING STARTED

1

Get alerted
